viscid.plot.cmap_tools.register_cmap(name, colors, reverse=False)[source]¶

Register a cmap given a name and either a cmap or an Nx3 or Nx4 ndarray or rgb(a) data.

Calls to_linear_cmap(colors) before registering.

Parameters:
  • name (str, None) – if None, name = colors.name; this will not play nicely with an ndarray
  • colors (Colormap or ndarray) – colors as either a matplotlib Colormap or an Nx3 or Nx4 ndarray or rgb(a) data
  • reverse (bool) – flip the lower and upper ends of the mapping
